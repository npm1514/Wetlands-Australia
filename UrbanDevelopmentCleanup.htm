Urban Development Cleanup

Clean Up Australia Day is Australia's largest community-based environmental event in 2014 close to 600,000 people donned gloves and picked up an estimated 288,650 tonnes of rubbish. This rubbish is usually the hard-to-get-to kind that would otherwise have ended up in our waterways, tangled up in plants, or killing wildlife.
